CSE 471 - Project 1 - Effects

Sample Xml Configuration

Effects are added on as attributes tto an instrument:

    <instrument instrument="Organ" chorus="0.6" flange="0.3" compression="0.1" noisegate="0.2">
  </instrument>

Effects implemented

  1. Noise Gate

    Attenuates signals below a threshold value , here threshold value is 0.025.

  2. Compressor

    Attenuates signals above a threshold value , here threshold value is 0.025.

  3. Chorus

    mixes audio samples with delayed copies of itself here, delay = 20ms-30ms and the frequency of the LFO = 1 Hz

  4. Flange

    mixes audio samples with delayed copies of itself here, delay = 5ms-10ms and the frequency of the LFO = 0.5 Hz
